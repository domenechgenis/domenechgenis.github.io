Role: Solo Developer

Concept: Tajapp, a versatile, multiplatform entertainment application developed independently during the challenging times of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Development: Leveraged Unity Ads and In-App Purchases (IAP) to create a rentable application, resulting in over 90,000 downloads and a diverse user base.

Technical Expertise: Utilized Unity Ads, IAP, SQLite, and Firebase to craft a user-engaging and monetizable platform accessible across various devices.

Unique Selling Point: Offering an immersive entertainment experience while implementing a sustainable revenue model through ads and in-app purchases.

Project Development Highlights

Development Emphasis: I independently designed, developed, and maintained it, refining expertise in cross-platform development and user retention strategies.

Monetization Strategy: Strategically integrated IAP and Unity Ads to offer users both free and premium content, ensuring a sustainable revenue stream.

Optimization: Continuously optimized user experience and application performance through iterations based on user feedback and analytics.

Conclusion

In essence, Tajapp stands as proof of my initiative and dedication, offering a multiplatform entertainment application. With over 3,000 monthly active users (MAU), it not only gained substantial downloads but also functions as a rentable platform. It showcases potential for innovative and sustainable monetization models in the app industry.
