Role: C# Unity Developer - Mobile Developer

Leveraging C# programming skills and Unity expertise for the development and enhancement to optimize the International Basketball Manager 23, focusing on seamless mobile integration and enhanced user experience based on feedback from the IBM 22 project.

Collaborating closely with the game design department to seamlessly integrate intricate gameplay mechanics and functionalities, meticulously porting the game to mobile platforms and ensuring optimal performance and user engagement.

Implementing a user-friendly creation mod system to make mods, integrating them seamlessly into gameplay, thereby enriching the gaming experience and fostering community participation.

Utilizing Jira to generate and manage development tickets, ensuring comprehensive execution of the outlined GDDs, and active participation in a cohesive development environment.

Contribution Highlights

Implementing and innovating feedback-driven features and mechanics to enhance gameplay, focusing on mobile optimization without compromising the game's core experience.

Collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to streamline the porting process, ensuring a cohesive and engaging experience across various platforms.

Introducing the Steam Workshop and a simplified mod creation system, empowering users to expand the game's content effortlessly.

Optimized SQL queries to enhance database performance, ensuring efficient data retrieval and management for the International Basketball Manager 23.

Developed Unity Editor Tools to streamline and enhance the game development process, improving the efficiency and overall workflow for the team.

Conclusion

In summary, I take pride in contributing my expertise to the International Basketball Manager 23 project. I'm enthusiastic about the future of this project and the opportunities for further innovation in the gaming industry.
