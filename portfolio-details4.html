Background of the Company

Fisherman’s Games is a studio founded by four university roommates and I. It all began as part of our final thesis. Our project won the best project of the year at the university gained recognition at events such as Indie Dev Day and Gamescom.

Project Solar originated from our collective effort, emerging as a result of one of these collaborations. It signifies the culmination of our combined skills, creativity, and teamwork, turning an initial concept into a realized project.
